## Review workflow

Go through `Tara_Content_Review.pdf` part by part. Per chunk/entry: confirm as
is, correct the text/table cell, or reject. Leave confirmed items at
`reviewed: True`; flip anything corrected-in-place back to `False` until the
fix is re-checked, and delete anything rejected outright. `git blame` records
who/when changed a flag.
